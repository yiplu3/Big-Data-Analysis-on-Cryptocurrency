Executive Summary

  • People who post the Reddit questions care about buying, investing and selling cryptocurrencies, and commenters tend to answer those questions in a professional way such as discuss the technologies, protocols, frameworks about cryptocurrency.
  • People are confident and optimistic about prominent digital currency titles such as Bitcoin and Ethereum as well as their supporting technologies.
  • Bitcoin led to heated discussions with its price surging before November 2021. However, people gradually lost interest in Bitcoin afterwards.

Data Analysis Report

Business goal 5:

With the popularity of cryptocurrencies, what are the most important topics or factors people care about regarding cryptocurrencies?

Technical proposal:

First split the sentences into words, stem, and delete stopwords. Then for submissions, count the frequency of words in the topics. For comments, use CountVectorizer to count the number of words with real meanings. Finally, use TF-IDF to find the words’ importance and extract the most important words.

  • Submission

Figure 5: Wordcloud

From the wordcloud on the most common words in the submission topics, we can see that users who post the Reddit submissions under cryptocurrency have significant concerns about topics such as "bitcoin","cryptocurrencies","eth" and so on. Although with the popularity of different cryptocurrencies in recent years, such as Dogecoin, Ethereum, Bitcoin still attracts extensive attention from the public. From the most common words shown above, the main purpose of users posting the submissions is to buy, invest, and sell cryptocurrencies since words like "price","market", "wallet" are of great amount in the topics.

  • Comment

Figure 6: Wordcloud

From the wordcloud on the most important words of the comment body, we can find that users who comment under the topic of cryptocurrency are likely to bring up words such as "pow","fork","pop" and so on. Different from the users who come up with the question about the prices of cryptocurrencies in the submissions, commenters tend to give replies in a more professional way, such as discussing about different protocols and crypto terminologies like PoW, Algorand, and Polygon. Just as the "art" shown in the wordcloud, in about 20 words (see from the distribution of the comments from cryptocurrency, the majority of comment length lies below 20), commenters describe cryptocurrencies as arts while the question posters see them as products with values.

Business goal 6:

What’s people’s attitude on Reddit posts and comments that mention the buzzwords?

Technical proposal:

Reuse the most frequent words acquired earlier, and filter for the sentences that contain the buzzwords. Then perform sentiment analysis on the corpus. Also, do the data visualization of the sentimental distribution on most important words.

  • Cryptocurrency Terms

Figure 7: The Sentiment Proportion for Common Cryptocurrency Terms

Based on the sentiment analysis on Reddit comments, we were able to obtain people’s attitudes (positive/neutral/negative) on various important concepts and terminologies related to cryptocurrency, based on the assumption that a sentence containing a keyword will also be discussing that keyword. To better understand how people’s attitudes are divided, we selected a few keywords according to their importance ranking based on TF-IDF. From the pie charts, we can see that comments are generally positive towards “PoW” and “fork”. PoW stands for Proof of Work, which refers to a form of cryptographic proof in which one party proves to others that a certain amount of a specific computational effort has been expended. On the other hand, Fork is a technical phenomenon that occurs when a blockchain splits into two separate branches whenever a community makes a change to the blockchain’s protocol or basic set of rules. Surprisingly, the majority of comments are negative towards the general term “cryptocurrency”. “Jargon”, which is a composite category that combines the results of the previous three keywords shows a slightly negative attitude, mostly due to the negative attitude towards “cryptocurrency” in general. We reckon that people associate negative emotions with “cryptocurrency” because conversations related to this topic are highly likely to involve discussion of trading and currency price, which is quite volatile and may lead to dissatisfaction.

  • Cryptocurrencies and Protocols

Figure 8: The Sentiment Proportion for Cryptocurrencies and Protocols

We also selected a few keywords about digital currency titles and protocols with high importance based on TF-IDF. Apparently, comments are mostly positive about Bitcoin and Ethereum, which indicates that the two digital currencies are going quite strong over the last year. Moreover, the majority of comments are also positive towards protocols such as Polygon and Algorand, which suggests that people are confident and optimistic about the technology.

Business goal 7:

For one of the most popular branches of cryptocurrency, how does sentiment related to bitcoin change with respect to its price?

Technical proposal:

Use NLP to identify posts that mention bitcoins, or look at future forms of bitcoins. Perform sentiment analysis on posts and assign a positive or negative value to each post. Then count the net positive sentiment of posts for each day. Plot the net positive sentiments and bitcoin prices to see the correlation between them. To determine the market development potential of bitcoin as an emerging virtual currency.


Figure 9: Sentiment Changes with Bitcoin Prices

Through sentiment analysis and comparing its result with Bitcoin price from January 2021 to August 2022, we found that the number of net positive comments (number of positive comments - number of negative comments) is correlated with Bitcoin price as the characteristics of the price change are well captured. Based on the plot, we can see that before November 2021, the peaks in the number of net positive comments coincide with significant changes in Bitcoin price. Namely, during March and September 2021, the Bitcoin price was soaring, and so did the number of net positive comments. Also, in May 2021, as the Bitcoin price plunged, the number of net positive comments reached its all-time peak. This correlation could be justified since when an investment market such as Bitcoin experiences great fluctuations, it is reasonable that people are discussing it more as their investment might be at stake. Furthermore, a price increase doesn’t guarantee any profit, and neither does a price decrease guarantee any loss. So, as the outcome depends on the investment strategy, the fact that there will be winners and losers in the market at all times leads to synchronized trends in the number of net positive and negative comments.

However, the narrative seems to have changed since November 2021. Both the number of positive and negative comments decreased significantly as the price of Bitcoin also fell precipitously. Normally, as the price plunges, the number of comments should increase, but it is not been observed from November 2021 and on. We reckon that it is because people were gradually losing faith in Bitcoin as the prospect of rising interest rates and reduced liquidity in the financial markets have dropped Bitcoin’s price to an all-time low.
